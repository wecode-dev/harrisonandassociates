PROCESS SERVING

Our Process Serving Solutions

At Harrison and Associates Ltd, we offer a wide range of services to meet the varied needs of our clients across the UK. From law firms to individual clients, our expert team handles each assignment with accuracy, urgency, and confidentiality.


Process Serving

We specialize in the delivery of legal documents, including:

  • Court Orders
  • Summons and Subpoenas
  • Divorce Papers
  • Injunctions
  • Writs
  • Statutory Demands











Fast, Reliable, and Professional Process Serving Nationwide

Our team ensures that every document is served in full compliance with UK laws and regulations. We provide regular updates, proof of service, and timely delivery.

Tracing Services

Need to locate an individual for legal proceedings? We provide professional tracing services to help locate individuals across the UK. Using advanced techniques and databases, we have a high success rate in finding those who are difficult to reach.


Document Collection and Filing

We provide professional document collection and filing services, ensuring that legal documents are delivered to the courts and appropriate parties without delay.


Eviction Support

We offer specialized assistance for property owners, helping with the legal paperwork and procedures necessary for tenant evictions.


Court Attendance

Our expert team is available to attend court to deliver affidavits of service or to serve documents in person if required.
