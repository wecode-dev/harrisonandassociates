About The Harrison & Associates Ltd

The Harrison & Associates Ltd is a private investigation agency based in London operating locally, nationally and internationally.

We are committed to providing our clients with an exceptional level of legal support and investigation services. We always aim to deliver positive results. To learn more about us, get in touch today.

  • Client Focused - The majority of our new clients come from existing client recommendations.
  • Discretion Assured - Discretion and confidentiality are built into all our services.
  • Leading Providers - We are trusted, leading providers of information and legal support services.
  • Approachable and Understanding - Our clients feel at ease with us as we work with them to solve their problems.

Our highly skilled team come from a range of backgrounds including both ex-military and Police officers.

This gives us immediate access to a comprehensive skill set, making sure the right person is assigned to your case.

The principal of The Harrison & Associates Ltd, is to guarantee professionalism, discretion and confidentiality at all times.

We are registered with the Information Commissioner’s Office, operating within the Data Protection Act, demonstrating our commitment to securing client personal information.

The Harrison & Associates Ltd is also fully insured for Professional Indemnity and Public Liability.

Our team each hold the IQ level 3 Professional Investigator Award, recognised as the standard industry qualification for the proposed SIA regulation of Private Investigators.
