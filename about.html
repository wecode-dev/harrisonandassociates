About The Harrison and Associates Ltd

With over a decade of experience in the process-serving industry, Harrison and Associates Ltd has become one of the UK’s most trusted names in legal document delivery. We work closely with solicitors, businesses, government agencies, and individuals, ensuring that every document is served promptly, correctly, and professionally.

Our Mission: Our mission is to provide accurate, timely, and confidential process serving services to all of our clients, ensuring that their legal proceedings progress smoothly. We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and reliability.

Our Core Values:

  • Integrity: We adhere strictly to legal and ethical standards.
  • Efficiency: We serve documents quickly and keep clients informed every step of the way.
  • Confidentiality: We handle all legal matters with the utmost discretion.
  • Client-Centric: We provide tailored services that meet the unique needs of each client.
